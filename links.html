YACHT CLUBS

Royal Victoria Yacht Club
With over 120 years of rich history, the RVYC is the oldest yacht club in western Canada..

SidneyNorthSaanich Yacht Club
SNSYC was founded in 1981 by a group of individuals interested in boating. 

Capital City Yacht Club
The Capital City Yacht Club has an extensive history dating back to early World War II when the club was organized as a civil defense unit.

CFSA
The Canadian Forces Sailing Association (Esquimalt Squadron), located at Munroe Head on the north side of Esquimalt Harbour, supports recreation primarily for military personnel of CFB Esquimalt, their families and civilian members of DND, their dependants and a wide range of employees of Federal Government agencies.

Nanaimo Yacht Club
Established in 1931 to encourage the sport of yachting, to foster seamanship and navigation, and to provide and maintain a suitable clubhouse and moorage facilities for the use and recreation of its members.

Royal Vancouver Yacht Club
The Vancouver Yacht Club was formed in 1903, seventeen years after Vancouver was incorporated.The Royal Vancouver Yacht Club is one of the world’s premier yacht clubs. Our mission is to promote yachting, seamanship and fellowship among members. 

Campbell River Yacht Club
Our club is dedicated to bringing ENJOYMENT to all aspects of Boating. We are a FAMILY recreational organization that operates within a family budget. If YOU are interested in boating, you may be interested in becoming a member.

Comox Valley Yacht Club
The Blue Jeans Yacht Club

Ladysmith Yacht Club
SNSYC was founded in 1981 by a group of individuals interested in boating. 

Maple Bay Yacht Club
Since its inception in 1925, Maple Bay Yacht Club has served its members and the community by providing an affordable means to enjoy the many benefits of yachting and related water sports on our beautiful coast.

Salt Spring Island Sailing Club
The Saltspring Island Sailing Club sits amid one of the world’s finest, year-round sailing areas.

Schooner Cove Yacht Club
Situated between Nanaimo and Parksville, on the east coast of Vancouver Island, members of Schooner Cove Yacht Club enjoy racing and cruising for twelve months of the year.

Kamloops Sailing Association
Welcome to the Kamloops Sailing Association (KSA). Come join us for dingy and keelboat sailing, kiting, and windsurfing.  

Kelowna Yacht Club
At our lakefront location in downtown Kelowna, we provide our members with top-quality moorage in Canada's largest fresh-water marina, excellent food and beverage service, social events and opportunities to assist others in the community..

Penticton Yacht Club
The Penticton Yacht Club and Marina is located on the South end of Okanagan Lake. The facility, a not-for-profit society, boasts 450 berths for boats ranging in size from 16’ to 65’ and includes moorage for personal watercraft & visiting boaters.

Vernon Yacht Club
We are located at the North East arm of 89 mile long Okanagan Lake, and provide moorage facilities for both power and sail boats.
