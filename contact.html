How to find us

We have two convenient locations in Sidney and Vancouver. Drop-in and see us.


Driving to The Sidney Loft

Our modern loft and facilities are easy to find once you know the right turn off. We are just off the Pat Bay Highway in Sidney. Take McDonald Park Road until you reach a large 4 way stop. Go straight over and drive just past Sleggs Lumber (on the right). Ahead you will see U Haul and the junction pictured above. Turn right and follow the road 100 metres where it bears right to reveal a series of industrial units. We are half way along.
