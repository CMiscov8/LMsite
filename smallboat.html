Windsurfing and Kiteboarding

We are able to service and repair all brands of windsurfing sails. We are avid windsurfers, and have years of experience repairing damage to all types of sails. Wave, freeride, slalom or formula - we know them all. Call or come by, or send us some digital photos for a quote.

Before your sails come to us, make sure that you have washed them with fresh water and that they are clean and dry. Sand and salt badly damage our sewing machines. If your sails have sand on them we will have to charge you a cleaning fee before we can get started.

At Leitch and McBride we take every precaution to protect your sail investment.

For example, we do our best to maintain a clean working environment. This protects your sails (and our machines) from damage due to grit and sand.
