Frequently Asked Questions

+ expand all

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit Card, Check, Debit Card, Cash and INTERAC e-Transfer. We

accept credit cards in the form of Visa and MasterCard

What are your hours of operation?
The loft is open Monday thru Friday 9:00 to 5:00 and Saturdays by appointment.
Do I need to make an appointment to have my repair evaluated?

No appointment is needed for us to take a look at your repair job. Just drop by

during business hours and leave a bit of time to go over your sail with us.

If I purchase a new sail do I have to measure my boat?

We make every effort to measure every one of our customer’s boats. If distance is

an issue and we can’t travel to your boat we will work with you so that we get

accurate measurements using our measurement forms. Once we are happy with

the information and have compared it to our files we start the design process.

Do you repair all brands and types of sails?

Yes. We have the equipment and skills necessary to repair all types of sails

regardless of who built them and the construction.

Do you do any other types of sewing work?

We do get involved with special projects and industrial sewing. It’s best to contact

us to see if we are able to take on a project.
